Late summer reading 2023

The New Girl (2022)

"The New Girl" (2022) is a brisk mystery that focuses on strong-willed Lia, who slowly realizes there is something odd about her school after she witnesses apathetic behavior from the school staff and students when a girl is dragged away by campus security. The story has a predictable premise set in a California school, Draycott Academy, but uses its Asian American cast to tackle bullying, Asian cultural conflicts, and corruption. While occasionally uneven in tone and somewhat shallow, "The New Girl" does make for an entertaining read if you enjoy fast-paced high school mysteries with Asian and socio-economic overtones.
