Late summer reading 2023

What do you feel like reading?

Do you feel like reading about everday heroes? From young businesswomen to a rock legend to a clerk to a Maori performer, all the books on this list are about mostly unremarkable people placed in remarkable situations which they may or may not overcome. Whether fictional or real, the characters in these books are worth admiring for their humanity. 

All the books in the list are suitable for high school students ready to embark on a reading adventure.
