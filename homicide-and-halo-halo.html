Late summer reading 2023

 Homicide and Halo-Halo (2022)

"Homicide and Halo-Halo", despite its title, is mostly a romance/slice-of-life story with some crime/mystery elements. This book is part 2 of the Tita Rosie's Kitchen Mysteries series. In this iteration, a murder occurs during a beauty pageant, and former winner and amateur sleuth Lila decides to distract herself from her personal life by attempting to solve the mystery. Manansla weaves in and out a colorful cast of characters and keeps the dialogue and pacing breezy and light. As with other books in the series, Filipino food recipes are included. "Homicide and Halo-Halo" isn't a serious whodunit but a quick and entertaining read at best. While Lila's ethnic background and quirky attitude makes her a likable protagonist, readers might want to pick up the first book, "Arsenic and Adobo", to fully appreciate the character as this book also has her dealing with PTSD from that adventure.
